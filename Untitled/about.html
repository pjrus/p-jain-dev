About Me

Hey there! I'm Paarangat Jain. I'm a student, developer, and accessibility advocate with a passion for solving complex problems. My journey into the world of code started early and has grown into a full-blown passion, now driving my pursuit of a Bachelor of Computer Science (Mathematics Major) at Monash University.

Currently, I serve as the Project Coordinator at the Monash Assistive Technology Team (MATT). In this role, I lead the development of affordable tactile displays for the visually impaired, bridging the gap between hardware and software. I also spearheaded the creation of a React Native mobile application to interface with these devices, working with a stack that includes JavaScript, TypeScript, and Git.

Beyond my core work, I'm always exploring new tools. My technical playground includes Python for data analysis, PHP and MySQL for backend systems, and tinkering with Arduino for embedded projects.
